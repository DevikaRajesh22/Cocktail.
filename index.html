Our Story..

After a lifetime of love with food, drinks and everything related, we got tired of people asking us the same few questions, “What good restaurants are there?” and “What should I drink this weekend and how do I make it?” Figuring that there’s Yelp for everyone to see what people think of the fine eating establishments around, we knew we could help in the other area: drinking. Hence, we launched Cocktail.
